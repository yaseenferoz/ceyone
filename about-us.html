about us

Founder & President message

During my 25 years of working in several industries in different roles and capacities, I realised that there are multiple possibilities and opportunities for the taking. Moreover, it was during these times that I realised that many professionals working in a various type of organisations, right from a large company to a small one, are sometimes clueless about how they need to operate in certain situations. They tend to be especially confused and unable to understand what the best ways are to progress in the Corporate Social Responsibility and Sustainable Development area. It is with this in my mind that I set out on a journey to ensure that these organisations are able to outsource these activities to those who are in the position to not only help them come up with new ideas but connect them to already existing avenues in CSR & SD area. The result of this journey is what is front of you, i.e., The Ceyone Empowering Society (TCES). The end goal is to not only help companies in fields of CSR and SD, but also to help the society as a result of this.

Thus, I along with other people who are extremely passionate about helping the corporate community, along with helping the society we live in, from varied fields including management, sustainable development, finance & accounts, agriculture, and rural development, amongst others came together to form this society.

Ms. Gayatri Raghu
